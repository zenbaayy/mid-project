🦠 Diseases Library

Learn about common diseases, their causes, symptoms & treatments

Common
🤧

Common Cold

Cause: Rhinovirus infection
Symptoms: Runny nose, sneezing, sore throat, mild fever
Treatment: Rest, fluids, and over-the-counter medicines. Usually heals in 7-10 days.

Infection
🌡️

Flu (Influenza)

Cause: Influenza virus
Symptoms: High fever, body aches, fatigue, cough
Treatment: Antiviral drugs, rest, hydration. Vaccine available for prevention.

Chronic
❤️

Diabetes

Cause: Insulin deficiency or resistance
Symptoms: Frequent urination, thirst, fatigue, blurry vision
Treatment: Insulin therapy, diet control, regular exercise.

Chronic
🫀

Hypertension

Cause: Stress, poor diet, genetics
Symptoms: Headache, dizziness, chest pain, shortness of breath
Treatment: Medications, low-salt diet, exercise, stress reduction.

Infection
🦟

Malaria

Cause: Plasmodium parasite via mosquito bite
Symptoms: Fever, chills, sweating, vomiting
Treatment: Antimalarial drugs, mosquito nets, insect repellent.

Common
🤢

Food Poisoning

Cause: Contaminated food or water
Symptoms: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, stomach cramps
Treatment: Hydration, rest. See doctor if symptoms are severe.

Chronic
🫁

Asthma

Cause: Allergies, pollution, exercise, cold air
Symptoms: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, cough
Treatment: Inhalers, avoiding triggers, allergy medications.

Infection
🧫

Typhoid

Cause: Salmonella typhi bacteria via contaminated food/water
Symptoms: Prolonged fever, weakness, stomach pain, rash
Treatment: Antibiotics, rest, clean water. Vaccine available.

Common
🤕

Migraine

Cause: Stress, hormones, certain foods, dehydration
Symptoms: Severe headache, nausea, sensitivity to light/sound
Treatment: Pain relievers, rest in dark room, avoiding triggers.
