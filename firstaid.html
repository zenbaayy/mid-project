🩹 First Aid Guide

Step-by-step emergency first aid instructions

🩸
Bleeding / Cuts
  1. Wash your hands before touching the wound
  2. Apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth or bandage
  3. Keep pressure for 10–15 minutes without peeking
  4. If bleeding soaks through, add more cloth on top
  5. Once bleeding stops, clean wound gently with water
  6. Apply antiseptic and cover with a bandage
  7. Seek medical help if bleeding doesn't stop in 20 minutes
🔥
Burns
  1. Remove the person from the heat source immediately
  2. Cool the burn under cool (not cold) running water for 10–20 minutes
  3. Do NOT use ice, butter, or toothpaste on burns
  4. Remove jewelry near the burned area carefully
  5. Cover loosely with a sterile bandage or clean cloth
  6. Do NOT pop any blisters that form
  7. Seek emergency care for large or deep burns
😮‍💨
Choking
  1. Ask the person "Are you choking?" — if they can't speak, act fast
  2. Encourage them to cough forcefully if possible
  3. Give 5 firm back blows between shoulder blades
  4. If not resolved, perform 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver)
  5. Stand behind the person, arms around their waist
  6. Make a fist just above navel, push inward and upward sharply
  7. Repeat until object is dislodged or help arrives
💫
Fainting
  1. Lay the person flat on their back
  2. Elevate their legs about 12 inches above heart level
  3. Loosen any tight clothing around neck or waist
  4. Make sure they have fresh air, fan if possible
  5. Do NOT give water until they are fully awake
  6. If they don't regain consciousness within 1 minute, call emergency
  7. Check breathing and pulse regularly
🐍
Snake Bite
  1. Move away from the snake — do NOT try to catch it
  2. Keep the person calm and as still as possible
  3. Keep the bitten limb below heart level
  4. Remove rings, watches, tight clothing near the bite
  5. Do NOT suck out the venom or cut the wound
  6. Do NOT apply ice or tourniquet
  7. Rush to hospital immediately — time is critical!
❤️
Heart Attack (Basic CPR)
  1. Call emergency services (1122 in Pakistan) immediately
  2. Have the person sit or lie down comfortably
  3. Loosen tight clothing
  4. If unconscious and not breathing, begin CPR
  5. Place heel of hand on center of chest
  6. Press down hard and fast — 100–120 compressions per minute
  7. Continue until emergency services arrive
