💊 Medicines Info

Basic information about common medicines — uses, dosage & precautions

💊

Paracetamol (Panadol)

Painkiller

Uses: Fever, headache, mild pain, body aches
Common Dose: 500mg – 1000mg every 4-6 hours
Available as: Tablets, syrup, injection

⚠️ Do not exceed 4000mg per day. Avoid alcohol.
🔴

Ibuprofen (Brufen)

Painkiller

Uses: Pain, fever, inflammation, menstrual cramps
Common Dose: 200mg – 400mg every 6-8 hours
Available as: Tablets, syrup, gel

⚠️ Take with food. Avoid if stomach ulcers or kidney issues.
🧬

Amoxicillin

Antibiotic

Uses: Bacterial infections — throat, ear, chest, urinary tract
Common Dose: 250mg – 500mg every 8 hours
Available as: Capsules, syrup

⚠️ Complete full course. Do not use without doctor's prescription.
💉

Azithromycin (Zithromax)

Antibiotic

Uses: Respiratory infections, typhoid, skin infections
Common Dose: 500mg once daily for 3-5 days
Available as: Tablets, syrup

⚠️ Prescription required. May cause stomach upset.
🌟

Vitamin C

Vitamin

Uses: Immune system boost, cold prevention, skin health
Common Dose: 500mg – 1000mg daily
Available as: Tablets, effervescent, chewable

⚠️ High doses may cause stomach upset. Stay hydrated.
☀️

Vitamin D3

Vitamin

Uses: Bone health, immune support, calcium absorption
Common Dose: 400IU – 2000IU daily
Available as: Tablets, drops, injections

⚠️ Get blood test before taking high doses.
🤢

ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts)

Other

Uses: Dehydration from diarrhea, vomiting, fever
Common Dose: 1 sachet dissolved in 1 liter clean water
Available as: Sachets, ready-made solution

⚠️ Use clean/boiled water. Safe for all ages including children.
🫁

Salbutamol (Ventolin)

Other

Uses: Asthma, shortness of breath, bronchospasm
Common Dose: 1-2 puffs when needed
Available as: Inhaler, syrup, tablets

⚠️ Consult doctor for regular use. Overuse can worsen condition.
🧴

Antacid (Gaviscon/Maalox)

Other

Uses: Acidity, heartburn, indigestion, stomach pain
Common Dose: 10-20ml after meals and at bedtime
Available as: Liquid, chewable tablets

⚠️ Do not take within 2 hours of other medicines.
